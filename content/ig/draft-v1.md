## Is IG Safe?

**Safety Rating: 4.9/5**

Short answer: IG is one of the safest brokers in the world. That's not marketing copy — it's arithmetic. The company holds licenses from 11+ Tier-1 regulators, is listed on the London Stock Exchange (ticker: IGG.L), and has been in business since 1974.

Here's what "Tier-1" actually means. Financial regulators are ranked by the stringency of their oversight. Tier-1 regulators — the FCA, ASIC, BaFin, FINMA, and others — require brokers to segregate client funds, maintain minimum capital reserves, and submit to regular audits. IG doesn't just hold one of these licenses. It holds them on four continents.

| Regulator | Country | Tier | License |
|-----------|---------|------|---------|
| FCA | UK | 1 | 195355 |
| ASIC | Australia | 1 | 515106 |
| BaFin | Germany | 1 | 148759 |
| MAS | Singapore | 1 | CMS |

Beyond licensing, IG provides negative balance protection for retail clients — you can't lose more than your deposit. Client funds are held in segregated accounts at major banks, separate from IG's corporate money.

The publicly traded status adds another layer. As a FTSE 100 company, IG publishes audited financial statements, files with the LSE, and faces institutional investor scrutiny that private brokers don't. When a company has shareholders demanding transparency, the incentives align with your safety.

One thing worth noting: IG's risk disclaimer states that 71% of retail CFD accounts lose money. That number is required by regulation and is typical for the industry. It reflects the inherent risk of leveraged trading, not IG's execution quality.

## IG Fees

**Fees Rating: 4.5/5**

IG's fee structure splits into two categories: spread-only for forex and indices, and commission-based for share CFDs.

**Forex spreads** start at 0.6 pips on EUR/USD — that's the minimum during peak hours. The average across a full 24-hour period is 0.85 pips. During core London/New York overlap (13:00-17:00 UTC), you'll typically see spreads closer to the minimum. After hours, they widen. GBP/USD averages 1.40 pips, USD/JPY averages 0.97 pips.

**Share CFDs** carry commissions. US shares cost 2 cents per share with a $15 minimum per trade. UK shares are 0.10% with a £10 minimum. These are competitive but not the cheapest — Interactive Brokers undercuts on share commissions.

**Inactivity fee:** $18 per month after 24 consecutive months of no trading activity. Two years is a long grace period — most brokers charge after 12 months — but the monthly amount is higher than average.

**How does IG compare on fees?**

| Metric | IG | Saxo Bank | CMC Markets |
|--------|-----|-----------|-------------|
| EUR/USD Spread | 0.85 pips avg | 0.6 pips (Classic) | 0.65 pips |
| Min Deposit | $0 | $0 | $0 |
| Inactivity Fee | $18/mo (24 months) | Varies by region | £10/mo (12 months) |

Saxo Bank edges IG on raw EUR/USD spreads, but Saxo's pricing becomes less competitive on exotic pairs. CMC Markets sits between the two. All three offer zero minimum deposits on bank transfers.

For forex-only traders, IG's spreads are competitive but not the absolute cheapest. For multi-asset traders who want one account across forex, shares, indices, and options — the overall cost structure is hard to beat.

## Trading Platforms

**Platforms Rating: 4.8/5**

IG doesn't make you choose one platform. You get six: the proprietary web platform, ProRealTime, TradingView, MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and L2 Dealer for DMA access. That's the widest selection of any broker we've reviewed.

**IG's proprietary platform** is HTML5-based and runs in any browser. It supports customizable multi-chart workspaces, 28 technical indicators, 19 drawing tools, and integrated news feeds. It won't compete with ProRealTime on charting depth, but for everyday trading, it's fast and clean.

**ProRealTime** is where the advanced charting lives. Available free for active traders, it offers hundreds of indicators, backtesting capabilities, and automated trading via ProOrder. If charting is your primary workflow, this is the platform to use.

**TradingView integration** means you can execute IG trades directly from TradingView charts — no separate login, no copy-pasting. For traders who already live in TradingView, this removes friction entirely.

**MT4 and MT5** are available for those who need Expert Advisors or have existing EA libraries. MT5 adds depth of market and more order types.

**L2 Dealer** provides DMA (Direct Market Access) for forex and shares. DMA forex spreads average 0.165 pips on EUR/USD — significantly tighter than the standard account. You pay commission instead.

## How to Open an Account

Opening an IG account takes 5-10 minutes. Here's the process:

1. **Visit ig.com** and click "Create account"
2. **Fill in personal details** — name, email, country of residence
3. **Complete the suitability questionnaire** — trading experience, financial situation, understanding of leveraged products
4. **Upload identity documents** — passport or national ID plus proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
5. **Fund your account** — $0 minimum via bank transfer, ~$450 minimum via card or PayPal
6. **Start trading** — demo account available immediately, live account after verification (usually same day)

The suitability questionnaire is a regulatory requirement, not a marketing exercise. IG is legally required to assess whether leveraged products are appropriate for you. Answer honestly — providing false information can lead to account restrictions later.

Verification typically completes within 24 hours. Some jurisdictions require additional documentation.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is IG a safe broker?

Yes. IG is regulated by 11+ Tier-1 financial authorities including the FCA (UK), ASIC (Australia), BaFin (Germany), and MAS (Singapore). The company is publicly listed on the London Stock Exchange as part of the FTSE 100 index and has been operating since 1974. Client funds are held in segregated accounts, and retail clients receive negative balance protection.

### What is the minimum deposit for IG?

$0 for bank transfers. Card and PayPal deposits require approximately $450. There is no minimum trading balance — once funded, you can trade any available market.

### How much does it cost to trade forex on IG?

Forex trading on IG is commission-free with spread-only pricing. EUR/USD spreads start at 0.6 pips during peak hours, with a 24-hour average of 0.85 pips. For tighter spreads, IG's DMA service (Forex Direct via L2 Dealer) offers average EUR/USD spreads of 0.165 pips plus commission.

### Does IG charge an inactivity fee?

Yes. IG charges $18 per month after 24 consecutive months of no trading activity. The grace period is longer than most brokers (typically 12 months), but the monthly charge is higher than average. Any single trade resets the 24-month counter.

### What platforms does IG offer?

IG offers six trading platforms: its proprietary web platform, ProRealTime (advanced charting), TradingView (integrated execution), MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and L2 Dealer (DMA). All platforms are available to every IG client at no additional cost, though ProRealTime requires a minimum number of trades per month to remain free.

### Can I trade crypto on IG?

Yes. IG offers 55+ cryptocurrency tokens as CFDs. This means you don't own the underlying crypto — you trade on price movements. Crypto CFD spreads are wider than specialist crypto exchanges, but IG's regulatory protections (negative balance protection, segregated funds) add safety that unregulated exchanges don't provide.
